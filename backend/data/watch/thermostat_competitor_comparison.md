## Demand Response Program Comparison

### Google Nest – Rush Hour Rewards
- Largest residential DR program in the US
- Partnerships with 100+ utilities across US and Canada
- In 2024, participants received over $40 million in total rewards
- Pre-cooling strategy used before events

### Ecobee – eco+ Feature
- Integrates directly with utility DR programs
- Enrollment bonuses and ongoing credits vary by utility
- Similar event structure to Nest

### Honeywell Home – Resideo Platform
- Connects to utility programs via the Resideo platform
- Amazon Smart Thermostat also uses Resideo/Honeywell technology underneath

### Amazon Smart Thermostat
- Enrollment via Alexa app → Energy Savings Incentives
- Works with participating utilities per ZIP code
- Limited to one program per household
- Full manual override during events

---

## Key Takeaway

For pure demand response value at the lowest upfront cost, the **Amazon Smart Thermostat at $59.99** is the most cost-effective entry point — especially when utility rebates ($50–$85) can bring the effective out-of-pocket cost to nearly $0.

For households wanting more automation, learning capabilities, and broader DR partnerships, **Google Nest** leads the market. For Apple HomeKit users, **Ecobee** is the best fit.
