# Google Nest Thermostat – Demand Response & Rush Hour Rewards Guide

## What Is Rush Hour Rewards?

Rush Hour Rewards is Google Nest's demand response program, powered by **Renew Home** in partnership with Google Nest and your local energy provider. It encourages participants to reduce energy usage during peak demand periods — called "energy rush hours" — when everyone in an area turns on heating or cooling at once, straining the grid.

Participants collectively form a **"virtual power plant"**, helping lower overall energy costs for utilities and consumers while earning rewards and bill credits.

---

## How It Works

1. **Enroll** via the Google Home app or Nest Thermostat → Settings → Energy Programs
2. Your utility predicts a large rise in electricity demand (e.g., heatwave, cold snap)
3. A Rush Hour event is scheduled and sent to all enrolled Nest thermostats
4. You receive a **notification on the thermostat display and in the app** with event start/end times
   - If the Rush Hour is in the morning → notified the night before
   - If in the afternoon → notified at least one hour before
5. Your Nest automatically adjusts temperature — pre-cooling or pre-heating before the event, then making small adjustments during
6. You earn rewards for participating

> **Full override available at any time** — simply change the temperature manually. 84% of participants reported feeling just as comfortable during events.

---

## Temperature Adjustments During Events

- Typical adjustment: up to **4°F (or 1°C)**
- Duration: typically **3 to 4 hours** per event
- Strategy: pre-cooling/pre-heating before the event window to minimize discomfort during
- The algorithm considers your past schedule and preferences to minimize impact

## Summer vs Winter Rush Hours

| Aspect | Summer | Winter |
|---|---|---|
| Trigger | Heatwave / AC demand | Cold snap / heating demand |
| Typical time | Afternoons / early evenings | Morning or afternoon |
| Rewards | Higher (more demand events) | Lower but available |
| System required | Any AC-capable 24V system | Electric heating (heat pump, electric furnace) |

## How to Enroll

1. Check if your energy provider participates: **nest.com/energy** or the Google Home app
2. Open the **Google Home app** → Your thermostat → Settings → Energy Programs
3. Or go to Settings → Energy Programs directly on the thermostat display
4. Follow enrollment steps for your utility provider

> Note: You'll need a Google/Nest account set up and the thermostat installed before enrolling.

---

## Nest's Other Demand Response Program: Seasonal Savings

In addition to Rush Hour Rewards, Nest also offers **Seasonal Savings** — a program that makes gradual, small temperature adjustments over several weeks to optimize energy usage based on seasonal patterns. It's fully automated and even less noticeable than Rush Hour events (89% of participants said they were just as comfortable).
